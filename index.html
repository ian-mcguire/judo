What is Judo?

Judo is a modern martial art, combat sport, and Olympic discipline that originated in Japan. Founded by Jigoro Kano in 1882, it emphasizes throws, pins, and submissions rather than strikes.

Why Learn Judo?

  • Build physical and mental discipline
  • Learn self-defense and body control
  • Explore Japanese culture and philosophy
