Core Principles

  • Seiryoku Zenyo – Maximum Efficiency
  • Jita Kyoei – Mutual Welfare and Benefit

Basic Techniques

Kodokan Judo includes three main categories of techniques:

  1. Nage-waza (Throwing techniques)
  2. Katame-waza (Grappling techniques)
  3. Atemi-waza (Striking techniques – taught only in kata)
